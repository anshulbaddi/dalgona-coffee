Dalgona Coffee

~ by chef anshul

this tasty coffee which is aslo called whipped coffee is a very famous coffee thats all over the internet.
it only requires four ingredients that you probably already have in your kitchen.

ingredients:

  • coffee powder
  • milk
  • sugar
  • hot water

process:

step 1: combine the coffee powder,hot water, and sugar
add 2 tablespoons of coffee powder, two tablespoons of sugar, and two tablespoons to a bowl

step 2: whisk the bowl of coffee with a handmixer or a whisk until hard peaks
using the hand mixer whisk up the coffee until hard peaks

step 3: serve with milk
put milk about 3/4 the way of the cup and then spoon on your whipped coffee and you can add coffee powder on top and you can mix it then you can enjoy
